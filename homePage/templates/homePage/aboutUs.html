    About Us

    Mission Statement

    Cocoon exists to redefine the apartment-finding process. We strive to raise the bar on quality of service, while simplifying every step of the way to be easier, quicker, and substantially less expensive. We achieve this by listening to our clients, working hard, always being open to criticism, thinking critically, researching thoroughly, testing everything, and innovating radically. We use radically new technology to break the glass ceiling that has defined real estate for years. Finding a new apartment is a big process. We help our tenants feel confident that they have found the best possible place to live.


    Why We Started

    The present market is swamped with both limited-utility, expensive brokerages, and with confusing, overwhelming technology companies that overload people with mostly unuseful information. Finding a place to live takes as long as before the internet, and nobody in real estate seems to care. Brokerages and tech companies alike profit hugely from poor service, and clients have no options for radically better service. We wanted to drop a bomb on the industry by doing the unheard of: top-tier service in far less time and for far less money.

    Our Solution

    We developed an intricate algorithm to allow you to indicate your desires and we will then match you with the home that best meets you needs. The survey will take a few minutes to fill out and in seconds a large list of homes ranked from the best match to less ideal matches will be displayed. Homes will be filtered on commute time, number of bedrooms, cost, move in time, etc. The survey allows for more or less customizations depending on how refined you want the search to be. You can add homes to your favorite list if you want to revisit them later. When you the home you want to visit, select the home and the options for contacting the agent will be shown! It is that easy!
