    About Us

    MISSION STATEMENT

    We are a digital brokerage who help people quickly find ideal homes. We guide our clients through the swamp of old, incomplete, redundant, and often fake information scattered across the internet by giving them an elaborate survey, which we then apply to all homes in market to eliminate and rank viable homes. Because we are a brokerage with the legwork of home-searching automated, we are able to help our users then get these apartments for less than anyone else in a fraction of the time.


    WHY WE STARTED

    After briefly entering the real estate market, we quickly realized the inefficiencies and the lack of innovation that is evident in the Industry. As giant corporations began to take over the market the desire for innovation dropped and the industry started stagnating. We want to develop a website that will make looking for a home more intuitive, safer and allow people like you to find the perfect match


    WHO WE ARE

    We are currently College Students who have struggled in the past with the real estate market in Boston. On top of our school assignments, we are working diligently to continue this project forward. We are ecstatic that you are visiting our website and hope that you find it an enjoyable and helpful experience!!


    OUR SOLUTION

    We developed an intricate algorithm to allow you to indicate your desires and we will then match you with the home that best meets you needs. The survey will take a few minutes to fill out and in seconds a large list of homes ranked from the best match to less ideal matches will be displayed. Homes will be filtered on commute time, number of bedrooms, cost, move in time, etc. The survey allows for more or less customizations depending on how refined you want the search to be. You can add homes to your favorite list if you want to revisit them later. When you the home you want to visit, select the home and the options for contacting the agent will be shown! It is that easy!


    WHAT YOU CAN DO

    Please reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns! We strive to best fit your needs and hope that you can help us improve! The real estate market is a daunting place and we strive to make the transition to your best ideal home that much easier!
